The Football Museum celebrates the passion for the most popular sport in the world. Located in a space dedicated to the past, present and future of football, this museum offers an exciting journey through the history of the sport, full of achievements and memorable memories, and is a testament to the importance of football in global culture and a tribute to the sporting heroes who dazzled crowds and continue to inspire the world.
